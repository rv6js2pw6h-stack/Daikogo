FAQ

Good questions.

What exactly is a daiko service?
Daiko (代行運転) is Japan's designated driver service. Two drivers come to you — one drives you home in your own car, the other follows in a second car to pick them up. Your car ends up at your door.
How is the price calculated?
Price = base fare (depends on distance zone) + per-km rate × distance + 5% service fee. We calculate it automatically using the straight-line distance between your pin and your drop-off. You see the full price before you book — and you pay your driver directly at the end of the ride, in cash or by card.
How long does a driver take to arrive?
Usually 20–45 minutes depending on time of night and location. You receive email confirmation with ETA within 10 minutes of booking.
How does the pin work?
Tap "📌 PIN" in the booking form — a map opens and you drag it so the pin lands on your exact location. This is transmitted to the driver so they find you precisely, even in parking lots or dark areas.
How does live tracking and chat work?
Once booked, a green dot appears on the map showing your driver's position, updating every few seconds. A direct chat opens so you can message them in English. For full real-time features, connect a free Firebase account — see the setup guide in the code comments.
Why do you need my ID?
Daiko drivers are responsible for your vehicle. We verify all users with a government ID to protect both drivers and passengers. Your document is encrypted and reviewed only by our trust & safety team.
What's the cancellation policy?
Free cancellation before driver dispatch. ¥1,000 cancellation fee after dispatch. No-shows are charged the full estimated fare.

💴 報酬はどう受け取りますか？
お客様が運行終了時に直接お支払いします（現金またはカード）。表示される料金が受け取る総額です。
🚗 代行ミッションの仕組みは？
お客様のお車を運転して目的地までお送りします。もう1名のドライバーが随伴してあなたを送り届けます。
⏱️ ミッションを逃した場合は？
ご心配なく — 別のミッションが自動的に表示されます。受信するには「稼働中」のままにしてください。
❌ 受諾したミッションをキャンセルできますか？
